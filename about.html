About PawHope

PawHope is a non-profit founded in 2018 to rescue, rehabilitate and rehome abandoned animals.

Mission

To rescue, rehabilitate and rehome abandoned animals while educating communities about responsible pet care.

Vision

A society where no animal suffers on the streets and every pet is valued as a family member.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about PawHope and our work

PawHope was founded in 2018 and has been actively rescuing and rehoming animals for over 6 years. We started as a small volunteer group and have grown into a registered non-profit organization.

We primarily serve the greater metropolitan area and surrounding regions. Our rescue operations cover urban and suburban areas where stray animal populations are most concentrated.

We welcome volunteers in various capacities including animal care, administrative work, fundraising, and event coordination. Visit our "Get Involved" page to fill out a volunteer application form.

Yes, PawHope is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. Our registration number is available upon request.

Donations directly support our rescue operations including veterinary care, food, shelter maintenance, and community education programs. 85% of all donations go directly to animal care services.
