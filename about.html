About PawHope

PawHope is a non-profit founded in 2018 to rescue, rehabilitate and rehome abandoned animals.

Mission

To rescue, rehabilitate and rehome abandoned animals while educating communities about responsible pet care.

Vision

A society where no animal suffers on the streets and every pet is valued as a family member.
